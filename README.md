# 🌿 ShiftChart
**Precision Medication Tracking for Modern Healthcare Professionals.**

ShiftChart is a high-end, premium utility designed to streamline the complex workflow of nurses and healthcare providers. Combining aesthetic elegance with surgical precision, it ensures you never miss a dose, an assessment, or a critical patient update.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🏥 Patient Management
*   **Dynamic Records**: Create and manage detailed patient profiles with unique identifiers.
*   **Status Indicators**: At-a-glance visual cues for patient stability and medication urgency.
*   **Encrypted Storage**: Local-first data architecture using Hive AES encryption for maximum privacy.

### 💊 Medication Mastery
*   **Smart Scheduling**: Plan dosages with recurring frequencies (Every 4h, 8h, daily, etc.).
*   **Overdue Alerts**: Bold, high-contrast visual warnings for missed or delayed medications.
*   **One-Tap Logging**: Quickly mark medications as "Given" with automatic timestamping.

### 🎨 Premium UI & Customization
*   **Designer Themes**:
    *   **ShiftChart Default**: Clean, clinic-ready aesthetics with soft greens.
    *   **Pinky**: Vibrant and warm tones for a personalized touch.
    *   **Hero**: High-energy blue and gold for the healthcare heroes.
    *   **Batman**: Ultra-dark, high-contrast mode for night shifts.
*   **Fluid Navigation**: Custom animated navigation bar with "liquid" stretching interactions.
*   **Accent Color Picker**: Fine-tune your interface with a curated palette of professional colors.

### 👁️ Wellness & Accessibility
*   **Eye Comfort Mode**: A systemic orange-tint filter to reduce digital eye strain during long shifts.
*   **Adaptive Dark Mode**: Seamlessly switches between light and dark environments.
*   **Haptic Feedback**: Premium vibration integration for tactile confirmation of critical actions.

### ⚙️ Comprehensive Settings
*   **Data Control**: Export your shift history or clear all records with secure confirmation.
*   **Notification Suite**: Toggle between Sound, Vibrate Only, or Silent modes.
*   **Internal Privacy**: View legal and support documents without ever leaving the app.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
*   **Framework**: Flutter (Material 3)
*   **State Management**: ValueNotifier / ValueListenableBuilder
*   **Persistence**: Hive (NoSQL, Encrypted)
*   **Animations**: flutter_animate
*   **Typography**: Google Fonts (Manrope)

---

## 🔒 Security & Privacy
ShiftChart is built on a **Zero-Server** philosophy. All patient data, medication logs, and personal settings are stored exclusively on your device. We use industry-standard AES encryption to ensure that sensitive medical information remains private and secure.
